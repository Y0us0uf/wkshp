Harley-Davidson Street Glide FLHX

The Harley-Davidson Street Glide Base is a touring style motorcycle and was carryover for 2018. Power is provided by a 4-Stroke, 1753.4cc, Oil cooled, OHV - Pushrod, V Twin engine with Electric starter. The engine is paired with a 6-Speed transmission and total fuel capacity is 6 gallons. The Street Glide Base rides on Aluminum wheels with a Dunlop Multi-Tread 130/60 R19 61H front tire and Dunlop Multi-Tread 180/55 R18 81H rear tire. The front suspension is a Telescopic Fork while the rear suspension is a Twin Sided Swing Arm.

Harley-Davidson Forty-Eight
